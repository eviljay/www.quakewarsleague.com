First custom maps appear!
Posted by: Salteh (Editor) - Sunday 27 April 2008, 16:53 Main
Beta versions of the first custom maps have been released a couple of weeks ago - Meltdown, Comm Link, Arctic Assault and Estate.

Meltdown and Comm Link are small 6v6 maps, designed for infantry only competition play. Comm Link even supports bots so you can practice on your own. The other two maps, Arctic Assault and Estate, both feature a long MCP section. Vehicles and turrets can be used on these maps.

For more information or download links, visit the map threads in the Community Site 'showcase your work' subforum.
