roYality Wins The Division B Final!
Posted by: bzZ_ (Admin) - Monday 8 October 2007, 22:36 Main
I was looking forward to this match, as both Afterlife and roYality were very tough teams to play against. So0o.. let's go!

Round 1:
Afterlife attacking, defending the Valley once more and rushed for the gap which used to be Bridge. They constructed in a very promising time: 18:57 mins.
Hence came the MCP and rolled out towards certain doom; roYality had set up a nasty defense including a very annoying Descecrator on top of the hill. It took Afterlife a lot of effort as well as lifes to finally deploy the damn thing; the Outpost was captured at 08:25 mins.
They immediately began their rush for the Shield Generator, struggled somehow but finally hacked it at: 02:50 mins. That's all roYality allowed from their opponents; no plant, Stroggification commencing.
roYality wins.
roYality now on the prowl, constructed at: 18:31 mins. The MCP was driven very defensively, as most of the team took control of the Outpost. A rather clear capture at: 14:36 mins.
Now roYality decided to do some sight-seeing, as the attackforce took the offroad route to the SG. This was very unexpected by Afterlife; almost no defense had been set up as most force had been deployed at the bridgeside. The nasty hackers had the time of their lifes: 12:47 mins. This meant that roYality had plenty of time to concentrate on the plant. It took some attacks though, but finally they managed to plant and destroy the objective at: 07:05 mins.
roYality wins.

Round 2:
Afterlife again dressed up as alien scum, trying to make life miserable of anything wearing camouflage. Still GDF managed to do some building: 18:49 mins. The Outpost was captured in a similar way as before; rather smooth: 14:38 mins. This time roYality choose the bridge, but Afterlife had set up a nice defense. I took a lot of rushes but they finally hacked the bugger: 09:46 mins. The plant though, wasn't that difficult. GDF locked down the place and blew it up at: 07:37 mins.
roYality wins.
Although both clans knew this game was played; they still played on for the fun of it. Great! Afterlife did manage to build the bridge very quick, but after that it was spamfest extravaganza.
roYality wins.

Summary:
roYality definitely had the upper hand in this one, although Afterlife had very nice actions as well. Fun match to watch, great teams, GG!

Congratulations to roYality eSports for being the winner of the match AND the winner of Division B.
Also congratulations to Afterlife with their second place!
Hope to see you guys on the Ladder very soon!
