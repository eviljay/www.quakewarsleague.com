4on4 infantry cup info
Posted by: Jasonii (Cup Admin) - Monday 12 May 2008, 23:46 Main
Like you know, the first matchweek has begun. For next 2 weeks, you should have played all 3 of your groupmatches. You need to YOURSELF arrange the match with your enemy. For any troubles, dont hesitate to contact an admin. We also got some servers to give out for a war, if neutral server cant be found otherwise.

We updated our database and webpages for this cup and we're sad to tell you that we encountered unexpected bug. Our webmaster(s) are working on the fix. For this perioid of time, you do not need to challenge through websites, but through IRC, so get in touch with your opponent, deal a date and save up the conversation logs for evidence. Play a game and BOTH teams need to report the score for any QWL admin. We hope this bug will be fixed shortly, and we can start using webpages.

The poules have been created - check them out from poles section and start fightning for the gold! Quakewarsleague wishes best of luck for everyone attending the cup!

Oh, and please idle&perform on #QWL channel on Quakenet, so your enemies and/or admins can get in touch with you. Thats the place to find an admin also, we're there for any questions.
