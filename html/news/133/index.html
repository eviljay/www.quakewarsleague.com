New Ladder Leader
Posted by: GlobalWar (Admin) - Monday 21 January 2008, 11:20 Main
Clan leaders can register their team via the Clan Management menu. Please make sure that you have at least 6 members linked to your team in order to register for the 6vs6 ladder.


There are some restrictions to keep in mind

You have to accept and play at least 2 matches in the QWL ladder per month. If you are found to decline challenges in order to stay top ranked you risk being deleted from the ladder.
Server CP and RCON stays in control of the QWL. If you are the top clan you will get admin access (not RCON) and we will change the si_name, si_website, si_irc, si_motd_1, si_motd_2 and si_motd_3 to reflect your clan.
si_motd_4 stays for our sponsor i3D.

If you are ranked one you have to claim the server at globalwar@quake-wars.nl providing info as in below example:
If you want colors in the text please add them also.

si_name "QuakeWarsLeague.com - Ranked Server #1"
si_website "www.quakewarsleague.com"
si_irc "#qwl on QuakeNet"
si_motd_1 "Welcome to the QuakeWarsLeague"
si_motd_2 "IRC #QWL (QuakeNet) www.quakewarsleague.com"
si_motd_3 "Public TS: teamspeak.i3d.nl:8908"


QWL is entitled to end/change this offer at any moment.

Good Luck!
