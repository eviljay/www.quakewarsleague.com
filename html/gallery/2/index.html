GDF Engineer
GDF Engineers are the most highly trained specialists in their chosen profession. Trained to offer support and provide basic defense, they are master tacticians and technologists.

Capable of constructing and repairing all GDF vehicles, deployables and weaponry, they are also experts in the assembly of machinery and large structures; making them invaluable to any objective-based mission. The engineer is also trained and authorized to deploy and maintain defense turrets, such as the Anti-Personnel, Anti-Missile and Anti-Vehicle deployables.

Completes Construction objectives and vehicle/deployable repair with Repair Tool, and deploys defense turrets and Landmines. Defuse enemy Plasma Charges to Defend Destruction Objectives.

Specialty:
  • Construct
  • Defend
  • Repair
Objective:
  • Construct
  • Defuse enemy Plasma Charges
Weapons:
  • Assault Rifle
  • Shotgun
Equipment:
  • Repair kit
  • Deploy Tool
  • Plant Mines
GDF Medic
GDF medical specialists are trained to provide necessary medical and combat support.

Medics are experts in their profession, able to heal and revive team mates by dispensing health packs and the use of standard issue Defibrillator pads.

Medics are authorized to order the air-drop of supply stations in the field.

Specialty:

  • Destroy spawnhosts
Weapons:

Equipment:

Strogg Oppressor
Strogg fire support experts, they can call in artillery deployables such as the Plasma Mortar, Rail Howitzer, and Orbital Laser, and can direct their fire against enemy targets.
